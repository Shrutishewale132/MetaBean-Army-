Physical Fitness Guide

Building soldiers of steel: endurance, strength, and mental resilience.

Endurance Training

Build cardiovascular strength with running, swimming, and obstacle courses.

Weekly Routine:

  • 5km run (Daily)
  • 10km rucksack march (Weekly)
  • Combat swimming (Bi-weekly)

Strength & Conditioning

Develop explosive power and functional strength for combat situations.

Key Exercises:

  • Deadlifts & Squats
  • Pull-ups & Push-ups
  • Kettlebell circuits

Mental Resilience

Train the mind for focus, stress management, and tactical decision-making.

Techniques:

  • Meditation & Breathwork
  • Stress inoculation training
  • Sleep optimization
