Help & FAQ

Find answers to frequently asked questions about joining, training, and career in the Indian Army.

Educational requirements vary by entry scheme. For NDA: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. For CDS: Graduation degree. For Technical Entries: Engineering degree in specified streams. For Soldier entries: 10th or 12th pass depending on the trade.

Age limits vary: NDA (16.5-19.5 years), CDS (19-24 years), Technical Entries (20-27 years), Agniveer (17.5-21 years), and other soldier entries (17.5-23 years). Age relaxation is provided for certain categories as per government rules.

The selection process typically includes: 1) Written examination, 2) SSB Interview (5-day process with psychological tests, group tasks, and personal interview), 3) Medical examination, and 4) Merit list preparation.

Yes, women are now being inducted into various combat roles. Initially through the Armed Forces Special Powers Act, women are now being commissioned into regiments like Corps of Engineers, Corps of Signals, and more recently into Artillery regiments.

Agniveer is a new scheme where soldiers are recruited for a four-year tenure. After completion, up to 25% may be retained for regular service based on performance. Agniveers receive training, salary, and a lump-sum "Seva Nidhi" package upon completion.
